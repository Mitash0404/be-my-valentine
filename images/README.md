# Add your photos here

Drop in 3–4 of your favourite pics (you + your girlfriend, or just moments you love).

**Suggested filenames** (so the page finds them automatically):
- `us1.jpg` — first photo (big one)
- `us2.jpg` — second
- `us3.jpg` — third
- `us4.jpg` — optional fourth

You can use `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, or `.png`. If you use different names, update the `src` in `index.html` in the "Our pics" section.
